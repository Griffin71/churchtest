About Us

Our History

Founded in 1954, St. Francis of Assisi Anglican Church has served the Mamelodi West community faithfully for 70 years. Rooted in the Anglican tradition, we are a church centered on worship, service, and growth in Christ.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To know Christ and make Him known through worship, teaching, fellowship, and outreach.

Vision: A spiritually mature, united, and empowered church transforming lives in Christ.
